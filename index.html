SCUDERIA FERRARI
LEGENDS OF SPEED
F1 HISTORY
Ferrari, founded in 1929 by Enzo Ferrari, began its F1 journey in 1950 — the first ever Formula One season. With iconic drivers like Michael Schumacher, Niki Lauda, and Kimi Räikkönen, Ferrari has achieved a legendary status in motorsport history. It's the most successful team in F1 with 16 Constructors' Championships and 15 Drivers' titles.

THE SCHUMACHER ERA
DOMINANCE
Between 2000 and 2004, Ferrari and Michael Schumacher dominated the grid with five consecutive World Championships. The F2002 and F2004 are considered two of the fastest and most successful F1 cars ever built.
SCUDERIA FERRARI
MODERN MACHINES
ENGINEERING
Ferrari continues to push the limits of innovation with hybrid technology and advanced aerodynamics. The SF90 and SF-23 represent Ferrari's effort to reclaim championship glory in the modern F1 era.
